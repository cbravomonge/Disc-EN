DISC Behavioral Styles Profile

Self-scored assessment for website use. Responses stay in the browser unless you add your own form handling or storage.

  1. Choose the setting in which your responses will be made: work, home, social, or another context.
  2. In each box, select the phrase that is most descriptive of you in that setting.
  3. In each box, select the phrase that is least descriptive of you in that setting.
  4. Choose only one Most and one Least response per box.
  5. Try to complete the assessment in about seven minutes.

Generate the results first, then download a PDF report. On Netlify, this should work normally.
